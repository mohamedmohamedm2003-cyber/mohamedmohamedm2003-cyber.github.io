About Me

I am Mohamed Mahmoud Ahmed, a civil engineer with a focus on geotechnical research and construction projects. Originally from Sudan, I am currently based in Indonesia, where I completed my final research on soil improvement and the effect of curing time on CBR values using Bacillus Tropicus.

I have hands-on experience supervising and participating in bored pile, column, beam, and slab construction, as well as conducting laboratory soil tests and structural analyses.

Passionate about infrastructure development and ground engineering, I aim to contribute to projects that are safe, sustainable, and technically sound.
